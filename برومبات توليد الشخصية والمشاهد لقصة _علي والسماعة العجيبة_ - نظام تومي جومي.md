# برومبات توليد الشخصية والمشاهد لقصة "علي والسماعة العجيبة" - نظام تومي جومي

## 1. برومبات توليد شخصية "علي" بأسلوب Pixar

بناءً على تحليل القصة، تم استخلاص السمات التالية لشخصية "علي":
- **العمر:** 7 سنوات
- **الجنس:** ذكر
- **السمات الجسدية والملابس:** يرتدي سترة ذات قلنسوة (Hoodie) يضعها فوق رأسه، ويرتدي نظارة طبية. طيب القلب، محب للطبيعة، فضولي.
- **النمط الفني:** Pixar

### 1.1. برومبت توليد الشخصية (Character Generation Prompt)

```
A highly detailed, 3D rendered portrait of a 7-year-old male child in the style of a modern Pixar animation. The child has short, neat brown hair, and bright, curious brown eyes behind round, dark-rimmed glasses. He is wearing a comfortable, slightly oversized green hoodie with the hood pulled up over his head. He has a kind, gentle smile and a thoughtful expression. The lighting is soft, cinematic, and magical, with vibrant colors and exaggerated, expressive, yet charming facial features. High quality, 8k resolution, masterpiece, octane render, unreal engine 5.

Negative Prompt: ugly, deformed, poorly drawn, realistic, photographic, anime, 2d, text, watermark, extra limbs, bad anatomy, old, adult, female, sad, angry, aggressive, dirty, messy hair, no glasses, no hoodie.
```

### 1.2. برومبت ورقة الشخصية (Character Sheet Prompt)

```
A comprehensive 3D character design model sheet of a 7-year-old male child in Pixar animation style. The sheet includes multiple angles: front view, side profile, three-quarter view, and back view. The character has short, neat brown hair, bright brown eyes, and is wearing a comfortable green hoodie with the hood up and round, dark-rimmed glasses. The background is a neutral, solid light gray color. The character's expressions are neutral but expressive. High resolution, clean lines, professional character design turnaround, 8k, highly detailed.

Negative Prompt: ugly, deformed, poorly drawn, realistic, photographic, anime, 2d, text, watermark, extra limbs, bad anatomy, old, adult, female, sad, angry, aggressive, dirty, messy hair, no glasses, no hoodie, multiple characters, busy background.
```
